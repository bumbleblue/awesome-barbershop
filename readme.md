## Getting Started

Resources to discover what barbershop music is about and how to dive in.

- [Barbershop music — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barbershop_music) — High-level overview of the style, history, and sound.


## Learn

Skill-building, arranging, directing, and educational materials.

- [Barbershop Harmony Society — Education](https://www.barbershop.org/education) — Courses, articles, and programs for singers, leaders, and educators.
- [Sweet Adelines International — Education](https://www.sweetadelines.com/education) — Learning resources focused on women’s barbershop.


## Organizations

Umbrella organizations and associations supporting the barbershop community.

- [Barbershop Harmony Society](https://www.barbershop.org) — The world’s largest barbershop organization.
- [Sweet Adelines International](https://www.sweetadelines.com) — Advancing women’s barbershop harmony through education and performance.
- [Harmony, Inc.](https://www.harmonyinc.org) — A vibrant community of women singers in the barbershop style.
- [Mixed Barbershop Harmony Association](https://www.mixedbarbershop.org) — Supporting mixed-voice barbershop ensembles.


## Repertoire & Arrangements

Sheet music, legal arrangements, and catalogs.

- [BHS Marketplace (Sheet Music)](https://shop.barbershop.org) — Official, licensed arrangements across levels and voicings.


## Media & Channels

Performances, interviews, podcasts, and videos.

- [Barbershop Harmony Society — YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@barbershopharmonysociety) — Performances, education, and event highlights.
- [Acaville Radio](https://www.acaville.org) — A cappella radio with frequent barbershop content.


## Events & Contests

Major contests, conventions, and gatherings.

- [Barbershop Harmony Society — Events](https://www.barbershop.org/events) — Conventions, contests, and community events.
- [Sweet Adelines — Events](https://www.sweetadelines.com/events) — Regional and international events and education.


## Communities

Places to meet other enthusiasts, ask questions, and share music.

- [r/barbershop](https://www.reddit.com/r/barbershop/) — Reddit community for barbershop singers and fans.
